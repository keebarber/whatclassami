# SCCA Quick Reference of Category Allowances

> Source: https://www.scca.com/downloads/44544-2019-03-08-quick-reference-of-category-allowances/download
> Fetched: 2026-07-04. Text extraction of the official PDF. Page numbers reference the Solo Rules.
> This is a guide only — verify against the official SCCA Solo Rules.

**SCCA's guidance:** "If it doesn't say you can, you can't." Allowances from Street generally carry over to Street Touring; Street and Street Touring over to Street Prepared; and so on — but not in all instances, and restrictions may change between categories (e.g., shocks are more restricted in Street than in ST/SP).

## Street Category

- Air filter element (no "cold air" intakes) — p.82
- Anti-roll/sway bar, ONLY 1 front OR 1 rear (+ end links) — p.80
- Battery and hold-down bracket(s) — p.81
- Brake pads/shoes — p.80
- "Comfort & convenience" items (gauges, lights, etc.) — p.75
- Exhaust, "cat"-back or axle-back — p.82
- Seat belt, restraint, harness for driver — p.76
- Shift knob — p.76
- Shocks/struts — p.78
- Spare tires, tools, etc. removed — p.76
- Tires, UTQG Treadwear 200 or higher — p.76
- Wheel alignment — p.81
- Wheel diameter change ±1" — p.78

## Street Touring Category

- Anti-roll (sway) bars, BOTH front AND rear — p.89

- Brake kits — p.88
- Brake ducts — p.89
- Bushings, suspension/steering — p.90
- Camber kits/bolts — p.91
- Cold air intake, up to throttle body or turbo inlet — p.93
- Engine accessory pulleys (EXCEPT supercharger) — p.94
- Engine radiator and fan(s) — p.95
- Engine and transmission mounts — p.95
- Exhaust headers and high-flow "cats" — p.93
- Limited slip differential, 1 only (none allowed in STS) — p.95
- PCM/ECU reprogrammed, OR fuel pressure regulator changed — p.94
- Pedal covers and "dress-up" items — p.86
- Seat(s), front — p.86
- Shift linkage kit — p.94
- Steering wheel — p.86
- Strut bars, tie bars (2-point only) — p.87
- Sub-frame connectors (bolt-on) e.g., Mustang, Camaro — p.87

- Tire width determined by class — p.88
- Valve timing controllers — p.94
- Wheel width determined by class, any diameter — p.88
